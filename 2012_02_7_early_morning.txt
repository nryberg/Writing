06:59 AM 02/07/2012

The early morning contrasts are deeper, brighter.  No soft greys for us, rather the greatest of ranges sweeping the harsh bus neon into the deepest tree shadows.  The world awaits, and we waken early.  Not happily.  Not always cheerfully, but sitting on this bus is a small token of our affection for being the first out the door, the first in the cube, and the last to get a clue that sleep is good.  Tonight, when the world pays attention to Leno and the news, we examine the interiors of our eyelids.
